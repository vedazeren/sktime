sktime.transformers.single_series.adapt¶

class sktime.transformers.single_series.adapt.SingleSeriesTransformAdaptor(transformer)[source][source]¶

Bases: sktime.transformers.single_series.base.BaseSingleSeriesTransformer

Adaptor for scikit-learn-like tabular transformers to single series setting.

This is useful for applying scikit-learn transformers to single series, but only works with transformers that do not require multiple instances for fitting.

Parameters
